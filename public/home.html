Helping Spousal Caregivers

HELLO

, the task is to design and develop a user-friendly web interface that could be linked from the diidii website. This interface should cater to spousal caregivers, typically aged 70 or older, who are caring for a partner with dementia at home. Your primary users are individuals who may have limited experience with technology, information/education on dementia or dementia caregiving techniques.
